Common questions

How much does it cost to rekey a house?

Rekeying costs vary depending on the number of locks. On average, it ranges from $15 to $30 per lock. We provide a free estimate before starting.

Can you install a smart lock on any door?

Most standard doors can accommodate smart locks. Our technicians will assess your door and recommend compatible options.
