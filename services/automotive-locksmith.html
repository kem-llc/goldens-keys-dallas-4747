Overview

Locked out of your car or lost your keys? Our automotive locksmiths can help. We specialize in unlocking cars, replacing lost keys, and programming transponder keys and fobs for all makes and models. We also repair ignition switches and extract broken keys.

What's included

  • Quick car lockout resolution
  • Key duplication and replacement
  • Transponder key and fob programming

How it works

1

Vehicle Assessment

We identify your vehicle's make, model, and year to determine the best method for key cutting or programming.

2

Key Generation

Using advanced equipment, we cut and program a new key or fob on-site.

3

Testing & Handover

We test the new key to ensure it works perfectly and provide any additional tips for your vehicle's security.

Common questions

Can you make a key for my car if I don't have the original?

Yes, we can create a new key using your vehicle's VIN or by decoding the lock. Contact us for details.

How much does it cost to program a transponder key?

Prices vary by vehicle. On average, programming costs between $50 and $150. We provide a free quote.
