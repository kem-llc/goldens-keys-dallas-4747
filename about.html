Our story

Your Local Dallas Locksmith Since 1995

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable locksmith services to the Dallas community with integrity and transparency.

Golden's Keys was founded in 1995 by John Golden, a locksmith with a passion for security and customer service. What started as a small mobile operation has grown into one of Dallas's most trusted locksmith companies. John believed in showing up on time, giving fair prices, and doing the job right the first time. Those values still guide us today.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of Dallas residents and businesses with their lock and security needs. From emergency lockouts to complex commercial systems, our team has the experience and tools to handle any job. We're proud to be a local, family-owned business that treats every customer like a neighbor.
