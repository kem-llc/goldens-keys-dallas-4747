Our story

Over 40 Years of Locksmith Excellence in Dallas

To provide dependable, affordable, and professional locksmith services that keep our community safe and secure.

Golden's Keys was founded in 1985 by John Golden, a master locksmith with a passion for security and a commitment to customer service. Starting from a small shop on Garland Road, John built a reputation for honesty, reliability, and skilled craftsmanship. Over the years, the business grew through word-of-mouth as satisfied customers recommended our services to friends and neighbors.

Today, Golden's Keys continues John's legacy under the leadership of his daughter, Lisa Golden. We've expanded our team and services, but our core values remain the same: fast response, fair pricing, and quality work. Whether it's a simple key duplication or a complex security system installation, we treat every job with the same dedication and care.
